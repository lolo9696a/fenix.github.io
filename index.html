Born from Passion

Fenix emerged from a deep understanding of what live casino players truly want — genuine human connection, professional expertise, and immersive environments that feel like the world's best land-based casinos, available anywhere.

Founded by a team of iGaming veterans, Fenix has grown from a boutique studio consultancy into the industry's most comprehensive live dealer solution provider, operating studios across three continents.

The Fenix Team

Our 200+ certified professional dealers represent the finest talent in live gaming. Rigorously trained in game procedures, player engagement, and on-camera presence, they are the face of your brand in every session.

Multilingual fluency across 48 languages gives Fenix the ability to speak directly to players in every major market worldwide.

Our Technology

Fenix operates proprietary streaming infrastructure built from the ground up for live casino environments. From 4K camera arrays to AI-assisted dealer scheduling, every element of our technology stack is purpose-built for performance and reliability.

We deliver 99% uptime guarantees backed by multi-CDN redundancy and real-time monitoring across all active studios.

To make world-class live dealer experiences accessible to every casino operator — regardless of size, geography, or budget. We believe the future of casino gaming is live, immersive, and human.

FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Fenix. Can't find the answer? Contact us directly.

General
What is Fenix Live Dealer Studio?
+
Fenix is a full-service live dealer studio management company. We design, build, staff, and operate professional live casino environments for iGaming operators worldwide — providing everything from custom table design and 4K streaming technology to certified multilingual dealers.
What services does Fenix offer?
+
Our core services include: Full Studio Management, Customised Table Design, Live Streaming Software, Professional Dealer Staffing, Multilingual Dealer Programs, Studio Design & Atmosphere, and Complete Studio Build-Outs. We can serve as an end-to-end partner or support specific elements of your operation.
How many languages do your dealers speak?
+
Our dealer talent pool covers 48+ languages, including English, Mandarin, Arabic, Portuguese, Spanish, French, German, Russian, and many more. We can match native-speaking dealers to your target markets with flexible scheduling.
What streaming quality does Fenix provide?
+
We deliver 4K Ultra HD streaming with ultra-low latency through our proprietary software infrastructure. Our multi-CDN architecture ensures 99% uptime with automatic failover, and our streams integrate seamlessly with all major casino platform providers.
Can Fenix build a studio from scratch?
+
Absolutely. Our Full Studio Build-Out service covers everything from floor planning and architectural fit-out to custom table installation, camera rigging, lighting design, and going live. We deliver a turnkey, broadcast-ready studio operation.
Where are your studios located?
+
Fenix operates studios across three continents, with headquarters in Malta and Tbilisi, Georgia. We can partner with operators globally and assist with local regulatory compliance in all major licensed jurisdictions.
Do you offer a demo or consultation?
+
Yes. We offer virtual studio tours and in-person consultations for qualified operators. Use the Book a Demo button to schedule a session with our team and we'll walk you through our capabilities, infrastructure, and how we can serve your specific requirements.
What is Fenix's uptime guarantee?
+
We guarantee 99% uptime across all active studio feeds, backed by multi-CDN redundancy, 24/7 technical monitoring, and rapid-response support. SLAs are customised per partnership agreement.
How do I get started with Fenix?
+
Simply reach out through our Contact page or click "Book a Demo" anywhere on the site. Our partnerships team will respond within 24 hours to schedule a discovery call and understand your studio requirements.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is available 24/7 for existing clients and within 24 hours for new inquiries.
