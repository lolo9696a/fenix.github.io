Born from Passion

Fenix emerged from a deep understanding of what live casino players truly want — genuine human connection, professional expertise, and immersive environments that feel like the world's best land-based casinos, available anywhere.

Live Miracle

Our proprietary studio management platform, Live Miracle, is the operational backbone of every Fenix studio. It handles everything from dealer scheduling and performance analytics to stream monitoring and compliance reporting — in real time.

Live Miracle ensures every table, every shift, every stream meets the Fenix standard of perfection.
