FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between dedicated and shared tables?
+
Dedicated tables are built and operated exclusively for your brand — your custom set, your dealers, your environment. Shared tables are premium multi-operator environments where we apply your branding overlay. Dedicated offers the most immersive branded experience; shared tables are ideal for faster launch timelines and lower initial investment.
How long does it take to launch a studio?
+
Shared table access can be enabled within 1–2 weeks. Dedicated studio builds typically take 8–14 weeks depending on customization complexity, licensing requirements, and integration work. We have streamlined our process to be one of the fastest in the industry.
Is there a GGR share or revenue share model?
+
No. Fenix operates on a fixed monthly fee model — no GGR share, no hidden revenue percentages. You keep 100% of your players' losses and your cost structure is fully predictable, which is a major advantage as your player volumes grow.
What licenses and certifications do you hold?
+
Our studio operations comply with MGA, UKGC, and Georgian regulatory requirements. We work with certified testing labs for RNG and fair play verification. Our security infrastructure meets ISO 27001 standards. We are happy to provide full compliance documentation during your due diligence process.
Can I integrate Fenix with my existing platform?
+
Yes. We provide RESTful APIs, WebSocket feeds, and work with all major aggregators and platform providers. Our technical team handles integration end-to-end, and most integrations are completed within 2–4 weeks.
