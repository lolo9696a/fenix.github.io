Frequently Asked
Questions

General
What is Fenix Live Dealer Studio?
+
Fenix is a full-service live dealer studio management company. We design, build, staff, and operate professional live casino environments for iGaming operators worldwide — providing everything from custom table design and 4K streaming technology to certified multilingual dealers.
Where are your studios located?
+
Our flagship studios are in Malta and Tbilisi, Georgia. We also provide studio management services for operator-owned facilities across Europe, Asia, and the Americas.
What games do your dealers cover?
+
Our certified dealers are trained across all major live casino games: Blackjack, Roulette, Baccarat, Poker variants, Sic Bo, Dragon Tiger and game show formats.
How quickly can we go live?
+
For shared studio setups, onboarding typically takes 4–8 weeks. For dedicated studio build-outs, timelines range from 3–6 months depending on scope. We assign a dedicated project manager from day one.
Do you offer white-label solutions?
+
Yes. Our full white-label service means everything your players see — dealer uniforms, table graphics, studio branding and streaming interface — carries your brand identity exclusively.
What is Fenix's uptime guarantee?
+
We guarantee 99% uptime across all active studio feeds, backed by multi-CDN redundancy, 24/7 technical monitoring, and rapid-response support. SLAs are customised per partnership agreement.
How do I get started with Fenix?
+
Simply reach out through our Contact page or click "Book a Demo" anywhere on the site. Our partnerships team will respond within 24 hours to schedule a discovery call and understand your studio requirements.
